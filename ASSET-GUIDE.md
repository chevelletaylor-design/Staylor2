# Asset Guide for S. Taylor Bookkeeping

This guide tells you exactly what photos you need, how to name them, and how to upload them.

## 📸 Photo Requirements

| What it is | Filename | Where it appears | Size | Aspect |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Sophia's headshot | `sophia.jpg` | About section | 600×750px or larger | Portrait 4:5 |
| Custom logo (optional — site has a refined default) | `logo.svg` or `logo.png` | Header + footer | Square | 1:1 |
| Favicon (browser tab icon) | `favicon.png` | Browser tab | 64×64px | 1:1 |

## ✏️ Filename Rules

- ✅ **All lowercase** — `sophia.jpg` not `Sophia.JPG`
- ✅ **No spaces** — use hyphens: `sophia-taylor.jpg`
- ✅ **No special characters** — no apostrophes, ampersands, exclamation marks
- ✅ **Standard file types** — `.jpg` for photos, `.svg` or `.png` for logos

**Good:** `sophia.jpg`, `logo.svg`
**Bad:** `Sophia Taylor.JPG`, `sophia's photo.jpeg`

## 📏 Photo Size Tips

Big photos slow down your site on mobile. Before uploading:

1. **Resize** — use Preview (Mac) or Photos (iPhone) to set width to 1200px max
2. **Compress** — drop into [tinypng.com](https://tinypng.com) for free compression
3. Aim for files under 500KB each

## 📤 How to Upload Through the Admin Panel

1. Go to `[SITE URL]/admin/`
2. Log in
3. Click the section with the photo field — e.g., "👋 About The Practice" → "Owner Profile" → "Owner Photo"
4. Click the **📷 image upload field**
5. Drag your photo in or click to browse
6. Click **"Publish"**

Photo goes live on your site within 60 seconds.

## 🌸 Replacing the Default Logo

The site comes with a refined italic "S" monogram logo. To use a custom one:

1. Save as `logo.svg` (preferred) or `logo.png`
2. In admin panel → 🏷️ Brand & Logo → click Logo upload field
3. Upload your file
4. Publish

The site will automatically use your custom logo everywhere (header, footer).

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

**"Photo is blurry"** — Re-upload at 1200px minimum width.

**"Photo is sideways"** — Open in Preview → rotate → save → re-upload.

**"Photo uploaded but doesn't show"** — Wait 60 seconds for the site to rebuild. Hard refresh (Cmd+Shift+R).

**"Wrong file by mistake"** — Just upload the correct file again with the same name — it replaces the old one.
